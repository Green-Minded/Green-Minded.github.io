No FOOD without SOIL

The nation that destroys its soil destroys itself. 
-Franklin D. Roosevelt

There is no such thing as "away". When we throw anything away it must go somewhere. 
-Annie Leonard

The Earth's wound is visible to human's naked eye.

We see it through many ways and one of the most visible is  cracked soil. 


Soil pollution is the effect of polluted materials and mixing of toxins in the soil. Illegal dumping of waste is the biggest reason for soil pollution which can also affect the health of people.

Soil pollution can spread through polluted water absorbed by the soil. The chemicals used in agricultural activities is also a cause for this dillemma. Soil is part of food production , therefore by damaging it, we are more likely destroying the foundation of food chain.

Soil is needed to sustain living because it has always been there, but when it's completely damaged, what are we going to do?

Notice every single detail for it could be a huge factor in saving the planet.
