BE AN ECOWARRIOR! 

An Ecowarrior is someone who fights for our Earth's future.

As ecowarriors, we want our future generation to experience the authentic beauty of our nature. To achieve this, we need to unite in protecting and nourishing our planet. Ecowarriors aim to instill  the importance of taking care of nature not just in people's minds but also in their hearts.  You are welcome to join our ecowarrior family. As we grow, we want you to grow with us. 

  1. THINK GREEN - Think of ways on how to help the environment. Formulate new ideas on how to help the environment and in conserving its resources. 
  2. ACT YOUR GREEN THOUGHTS - Turn your ideas to reality. Try to bring life to your ideas and see if the community will embrace it.  
  3. LIVE A GREEN HABIT - Don't stop thinking on how to save the earth. While thinking of new ways on how to save it, just continue protecting it. Also, aim to inform other people about the situation of our planet now. 

VISION

Bring out the love for nature with the help of the movement.

MISSION

Create big change through small actions.

GOALS

Exercise different activities for the improvement of the surroundings.

ecobricks

This webpage will not be successful without the help of our partner in saving the Earth which is Ecobricks. 

Ecobricks is a non profit organization which collects bottles that have used food wrappers inside to make them solid. Ecobricks has ## drop off points here in the Philippines and is gradually increasing. Some of the bricks are already used in accomplishing the walls of public cr in Makati City, Philippines. This might be the start of something big in saving the earth, good job people!

However, Ecobricks is just a temporary solution. We should focus on the real problem: ourselves. People create waste therefore people should be the one who minimize it. People are the one who can think, know what's right from wrong. Use our minds in doing good things to our earth for we also benefit from having a healthy Earth.
